# Contrbuting
This repository is open to contribution under the following guidelines:

## Definitions
**Material** is defined as the informational substance or writing that is made available or is to be made available in an article.

**Source** is defined as the existing resource, whether internet-based, word-of-mouth quote, or otherwise that is being referenced by material.
 
**Target audience** is defined as the group of readers that an article is meant to be consumed (or read) by.

## Material guidelines
1. All material must be communicated in a manner that is consumable by the target audience.
2. All material must reflect the current state of the field and should only include historical references when denoting a specific change of standards.
    1. In this instance, the material must be clearly marked as historical and superseded.
3. All material must contain factual information that references to documented modern standards, guidelines, or resources.
4. All material must match its source's intended usage meaning.

## Source guidelines
1. Any source referenced must be an authoritative 3rd party of credible background who has demonstrated a key understanding and higher development of key facts surrounding an issue, posture, or topic.
2. Any source referenced must be up-to-date and reflect the current state of the field.
3. Any hyperlink referenced in the article must link directly to information sources that are free-of-charge and accessible.
